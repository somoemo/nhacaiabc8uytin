Xi dach la mot trong nhung tro choi duoc yeu thich nhat trong linh vuc ca cuoc truc tuyen. Voi cach choi don gian, quy tac ro rang va co hoi chien thang cao, xi dach da thu hut duoc rat nhieu nguoi tham gia. Neu ban la mot nguoi moi bat dau choi xi dach hoac dang tim hieu ve tro choi nay, bai viet sau day se cung cap cho ban nhung thong tin can thiet de hieu ro hon ve cach choi va cach thuc dat cuoc trong xi dach.

Quy tac co ban cua xi dach

Xi dach duoc choi boi 1 den nhieu nguoi, su dung bo bai gom 52 la. Muc tieu cua tro choi la dat duoc tong diem gan bang hoac bang 21 diem va vuot qua diem cua nha cai. Moi la bai co gia tri diem nhu sau:

  • Cac la bai tu 2 den 10 co gia tri tuong ung voi so diem duoc ghi tren la bai.
  • The J, Q, K co gia tri 10 diem.
  • The A co gia tri 1 hoac 11 diem, tuy thuoc vao tinh huong cua tro choi.

Tro choi bat dau bang viec nguoi choi dat cuoc va nhan duoc 2 la bai dau tien. Sau do, nguoi choi co the lua chon them the (hit) hoac khong (stand) de tang gia tri diem cua minh. Neu tong diem vuot qua 21, nguoi choi se thua cuoc va nha cai se gianh chien thang. Neu nguoi choi khong vuot qua 21 va co diem cao hon nha cai, nguoi choi se thang va nhan duoc tien cuoc tuong ung. Neu tong diem cua nguoi choi va nha cai bang nhau, tro choi se ket thuc voi ket qua hoa (push).



Cach dat cuoc trong xi dach

De tham gia vao tro choi xi dach, nguoi choi can dat cuoc truoc khi bat dau van choi. Nguoi choi co the dat cuoc tren nhieu o (co the la ca o cua nguoi choi va cua nha cai). Cac loai cuoc pho bien trong xi dach bao gom:

  • Cuoc tren o cua nguoi choi: Nguoi choi dat cuoc vao o cua minh va se thang neu tong diem cua nguoi choi cao hon nha cai.
  • Cuoc tren o cua nha cai: Nguoi choi dat cuoc vao o cua nha cai va se thang neu tong diem cua nha cai cao hon nguoi choi.
  • Cuoc chan/le: Nguoi choi dat cuoc vao tong diem cua 2 la bai dau tien co phai la so chan hay le hay khong.
  • Cuoc doi/suat: Nguoi choi dat cuoc vao tong diem cua 2 la bai dau tien co phai la cap hay khong.

Ngoai ra, con co nhieu loai cuoc khac nhu cuoc tong diem cua 3 la bai, cuoc tong diem cua nguoi choi va nha cai va cuoc tong diem cua tat ca cac la bai.

Mot so chien thuat co ban trong xi dach

De co co hoi chien thang cao hon trong xi dach, nguoi choi co the ap dung mot so chien thuat sau:

  • Phan tich the: Trong truong hop nha cai mo the la 2, 3, 4, 5 hoac 6, nguoi choi nen lua chon them the (hit) de tang gia tri diem cua minh.
  • Ngung lay the: Neu nguoi choi co tong diem cao hon 12, nen ngung lay them the de tranh vuot qua gia tri 21 va thua cuoc.
  • Phan tich tong diem: Nguoi choi nen lua chon cuoc tren tong diem cua 2 la bai dau tien neu nha cai mo the la 7 hoac cao hon.
  • Phan tich tong diem cua nha cai: Neu tong diem cua nha cai la 17 hoac cao hon, nguoi choi nen ngung lay them the de tang co hoi chien thang.

Kinh nghiem choi xi dach thanh cong

Ben canh viec ap dung cac chien thuat co ban, nguoi choi cung can luu y mot so kinh nghiem de co the choi xi dach thanh cong:

  • Dat muc cuoc phu hop: Nen dat cuoc voi so tien ma ban co the chap nhan de thua ma khong anh huong den tai chinh cua ban.
  • Dieu chinh chien thuat: Neu chien thuat hien tai khong mang lai ket qua nhu mong doi, hay thu ap dung mot chien thuat khac de tang co hoi chien thang.
  • Bo cuoc khi can thiet: Neu tong diem cua ban qua thap va khong con co hoi de thang, hay dat cuoc thap de giam thieu muc thua lo.
  • Chon nha cai uy tin: Chon nha cai co do tin cay cao va cung cap cac dich vu ho tro tot de dam bao su cong bang va an toan trong tro choi.
